Teacher Training Boot Camp

Over the course of 6 weeks you will learn the fundamentals of software development so you can teach software literacy in your own classrooms.

About us

Operation Spark is a New Orleans non-profit that teaches the fundamentals of software development in a fun and engaging environment. We’re opening a new door to prosperity for disconnected youth by presenting the fastest route to a career in software development.

Through our programs we progressively increase the skills of our students from novice to the level of internship. Part of our mission is to empower New Orleans schools with the literacy of software creation so we’re now offering courses tailored specifically for teachers in a K-12 environment.

What you'll learn

  • How to read and write JavaScript
  • Professional workflow of a software developer
  • Efficient debugging and problem solving
  • How to get around the command line terminal
  • Best methods for teaching children

What to expect

You’ll learn software development while using real tools, adopting best practices, and building actual web-based applications. Boot Camp takes the uninitiated to the novice level by ensuring they’re literate in JavaScript and HTML5 and capable of writing simple software apps.

By the end of Boot Camp, participants will know how to read and write JavaScript, and be familiar with the professional workflow of a software developer. Join Operation Spark’s Teacher Training program, and we’ll train you to teach your students to create compelling apps and games, and see you on your way to becoming a computer super user.

Our Teacher Training begins with a one day crash course, where we’ll speed you through the tools we’ll be using, our workflow, writing and stepping through code, debugging, general problem solving and how to communicate with us for support at anytime. You’ll be provided with curriculum and digital tools for managing the tasks and progress of each participant.

Following our crash course, we’ll see you once a week for a 2-3 hour session, where we’ll step through new lesson materials and support you through your experience in conducting your own Boot Camp. We’ll also support you virtually through our mentorship program and our online forum, where you can ask us questions, paste code, and our developers will respond with answers.


Join us for Operation Spark Teacher Training, teach your students to build apps, and be the hippest teacher on campus.

Boot camp deliverables

By end of camp, our participants are on their way to being super users, accessing computers via the command-line, reading from and persisting data to the file-system, and they leave literate in the basics of JavaScript having built several apps and games in Node.js and HTML5. They’ve learned to debug an app and problem solve on the Internet, and know where to find the resources to continue learning on their own or in teams. They also learn the soft skills that make them a great team player.
