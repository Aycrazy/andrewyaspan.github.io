We teach kids to code

Operation Spark is a program founded in September 2013 to propagate software development literacy throughout schools in New Orleans, and to prepare Opportunity Youth for a career in software development as quickly and cost effectively as possible.

Join Us


What we teach

1 Coding skills up to a level proficient enough to get a junior developer position at a startup

2 Visual design skills so that students know what good products look like and how to produce them

3 Life skills: public speaking, communication, managing stress, literacy, and more

4 Career development: working in teams, project management, marketing, and more
